FAQ

1. What is a StreetHack ?

StreetHack is a w phase 24-hour hackathon with a twist: our challenges are themed and well described here.

2. Count me in; how do I sign up ?

You can reigster here!

3. What do I bring ?

You'll need to bring your ID, a laptop, any device you think you will need, chargers, and your creativity!

4. Are there prizes for the hack categories ?

We are able to provide cash and software prizes thanks to our sponsors!

5. Wait, do I have to pay ?

Not at all; StreetHack is absolutely FREE! We are kindly funded by some awesome sponsors, and thanks to them, we can provide you with power, swag, and food, so you don't have to spend a penny once you're here. Unfortunately, we are not able to provide any travel reimbursements at this time.

6. I might get Hungry after a while.

Don't worry, food is on us, non-stop supply of snakcs and drinks

7. Will internet be provided ?

Continous high speed interent will be provided

8. Where exactly is the Hackathon ?

Pinned Location on Google Maps have been provided

9. Help! I don't have a team!

That's okay! If you don't have a team, just take part in the icebreaker event at the start of the hack; there, you can pitch your ideas to others, or join another to help build a great idea. It's a great place to meet people, but if you're more of a lone wolf, you're more than welcome to hack on your own, too!
